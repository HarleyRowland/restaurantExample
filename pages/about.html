About Us

Who We Are At Pizza You Like

At Pizza You Love, we have a team of experienced chefs who have over 30 years experience combined and we all aim to deliver the best food and service to our customers. We have tried to create a laid-back, fun atmosphere here which we hope will make you want to come back for more!

We are a group of friends who decided to open a restaurant after our long careers in the culinary industry. We all have a passion for food and, in particular, the Italian variety! Since opening in 2015, we have had great feedback from customers which has helped us to hone the service we provide to a high level.
